Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of rebar detailing and estimation services to support your construction projects with precision and efficiency.

Rebar Detailing (Shop Drawings)

Detailed shop drawings for rebar placement, ensuring accurate fabrication and installation. Compliant with US and Canadian standards including ACI 315, CSA A23.1, and local building codes.

  • 2D and 3D rebar detailing
  • Dimensioned placement drawings
  • Code compliance verification
  • Fabricator-friendly formats

Bar Bending Schedules (BBS)

Comprehensive schedules for bar bending, including quantities, shapes, and dimensions for efficient material procurement. Our BBS includes detailed mark numbers, bar sizes, lengths, and bending details.

  • Complete bar schedules
  • Shape codes and dimensions
  • Material quantity summaries
  • Excel and PDF formats

Rebar Estimation & Take-Offs

Precise estimation of rebar quantities and costs, helping you plan budgets and procurement effectively. Our detailed take-offs include material quantities, waste factors, and cost breakdowns.

  • Quantity take-offs from drawings
  • Material cost estimation
  • Labor cost analysis
  • Budget planning reports

IFC / RFIs Support

Support for Issued for Construction (IFC) drawings and Requests for Information (RFIs) to resolve on-site queries. We provide comprehensive documentation and quick response times to keep your projects on schedule.

  • IFC drawing preparation and review
  • RFI response and clarification
  • Field coordination support

As-Built Drawings

Accurate as-built documentation of rebar installations for record-keeping and future reference. Our detailed drawings ensure compliance and facilitate future maintenance and modifications.

  • Field measurement documentation
  • As-installed condition records
  • Compliance verification

Rebar Accessories Calculation

Calculation and specification of rebar accessories such as chairs, ties, and couplers for complete reinforcement solutions. We ensure all components are properly specified for optimal structural performance.

  • Accessory quantity take-offs
  • Material specification sheets
  • Cost estimation for accessories
